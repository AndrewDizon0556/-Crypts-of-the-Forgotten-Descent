GAME TITLE:
  Crypts of the Forgotten Descent

GENRE:
  Roguelike Dungeon Crawler (Turn-Based, Procedural, Permadeath)

LORE & SETTING:
  Deep beneath the ruins of the ancient city of Valdris lies a
  labyrinthine network of crypts — carved not by human hands, but
  by something far older. You are a Descender: a relic-hunter hired
  to recover the Shards of Ethos, seven cursed artifacts sealed
  in the deepest vault. No one who has entered has ever returned.
  You intend to be the first.

  Each floor you descend warps reality further. The deeper you go,
  the more the dungeon *remembers* you — and the harder it fights
  back.

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
CORE RULES
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[1] TURN SYSTEM
  - The game is strictly turn-based.
  - Each player action (move, attack, use item, wait) consumes 1 turn.
  - After each player turn, ALL living enemies take their turn.
  - Waiting (pressing SPACE) passes the turn and regenerates 1 HP.

[2] MOVEMENT
  - Player moves on a tile-based grid (1 tile per turn).
  - Movement directions: Up, Down, Left, Right (arrow keys or WASD).
  - Diagonal movement is NOT allowed.
  - Moving into an enemy tile initiates a melee attack automatically.
  - Moving into a wall tile does nothing (no turn consumed).

[3] COMBAT
  - Combat is automatic when the player walks into an enemy.
  - Damage Formula:  DMG = max(1, ATK - ENEMY_DEF + random(-1, 2))
  - Critical Hit: 10% chance to deal 2x damage.
  - Enemy attacks back immediately after the player's strike.
  - If HP reaches 0, the entity dies.

[4] PLAYER STATS
  - HP   : Hit Points. Reach 0 = death (permadeath applies).
  - ATK  : Attack power. Affects damage dealt.
  - DEF  : Defense. Reduces incoming damage.
  - LVL  : Player level. Increases with XP.
  - XP   : Experience Points. Gained by killing enemies.
  - GOLD : Currency. Used at shrine shops on certain floors.

[5] LEVELING UP
  - XP thresholds: Level N requires (N * 50) XP to reach.
  - On level up:
      → +10 Max HP (and restore 10 HP)
      → +2 ATK
      → +1 DEF
  - Maximum player level: 20.

[6] PERMADEATH
  - Death is permanent. The save file is deleted on death.
  - A new run generates a completely new dungeon seed.
  - Death screen shows: floors reached, enemies killed, gold found.

[7] DUNGEON FLOORS
  - The dungeon has 10 floors minimum (endless mode after floor 10).
  - Each floor is procedurally generated with a unique seed.
  - Floor difficulty scales every 2 floors:
      Floors 1-2  : Easy   (weak enemies, many items)
      Floors 3-4  : Normal (balanced)
      Floors 5-6  : Hard   (elite enemies appear)
      Floors 7-8  : Brutal (boss minions patrol)
      Floors 9-10 : Inferno (final boss floor at 10)

[8] FIELD OF VIEW (FOV)
  - Player can only see tiles within a radius of 6 tiles.
  - Explored tiles outside FOV appear as dim memory (dark gray).
  - Unexplored tiles remain completely hidden (black).
  - Enemies outside FOV do NOT take their turn (frozen until seen).

[9] WIN CONDITION
  - Reach Floor 10, defeat the Final Boss: The Hollow Warden.
  - Collect all 7 Shards of Ethos scattered across floors 4–10.
  - Escape via the Ascension Portal that opens after the boss dies.

[10] LOSE CONDITION
  - Player HP reaches 0 at any point.
  - No revives, no checkpoints, no second chances.

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ITEMS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  CONSUMABLES (single use, picked up automatically)
    Health Potion  [!]  → Restore 15 HP
    Mega Potion    [!!] → Restore 35 HP
    Bomb           [*]  → Deal 20 DMG to all adjacent enemies
    Scroll of Fire [~]  → Deal 12 DMG to all enemies on screen
    Antidote       [+]  → Cure Poison status

  EQUIPMENT (equip automatically on pickup, replacing old gear)
    Rusty Sword    [/]  ATK +2
    Iron Sword     [/]  ATK +4
    Shadow Blade   [/]  ATK +7, 15% lifesteal
    Leather Armor  [[]  DEF +2
    Chain Mail     [[]  DEF +4
    Obsidian Plate [[]  DEF +7, -1 ATK penalty

  SPECIAL
    Shard of Ethos [$]  → Quest item. Collect all 7 to unlock ending.
    Dungeon Key    [k]  → Opens locked rooms on the same floor.

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
STATUS EFFECTS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  POISON   : Lose 2 HP per turn for 5 turns. Cure with Antidote.
  SLOW     : Move/attack every other turn for 3 turns.
  BLEED    : Lose 1 HP per turn for 8 turns. Cured by any heal.
  CURSED   : ATK and DEF halved for 4 turns (from Lich enemies).

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
CONTROLS (Default)
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  WASD / Arrow Keys  : Move player
  SPACE              : Wait (skip turn, regen 1 HP)
  I                  : Open inventory
  E                  : Use/equip highlighted item
  M                  : Toggle minimap
  ESC                : Pause menu
  Q (in pause)       : Quit and save progress

DEVELOPER NOTES
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  Engine     : Python 3.10+ with pygame 2.x
  Map Size   : 60x40 tiles per floor
  Tile Size  : 16x16 pixels (scaled 2x on render)
  Save Format: JSON (single slot, auto-save per floor)
  RNG Seed   : Unix timestamp at run start (displayed on death screen)
